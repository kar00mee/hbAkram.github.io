With wearable tech becoming increasingly more prevalent, the numbe adults who use a wearable device has grown from 39.5 million back in 2015, to 81.7 million in 2018. As technology advances.

we see an increase in the human desire to have it integrated into our lives.,

It is this desire that has driven technology from bulky, stationary electronics, to sleek handheld devices, to this new wave of “wearable technology” that is becoming more apparent in the developed world. Wearable technology can be defined as electronic devices that can be worn as accessories, embedded in clothing, implanted in the user’s body, or even tattooed on the skin.
